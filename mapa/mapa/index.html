    INFRASTRUCTURE OF URBAN NON-HUMAN LIFE
    Pigeons & Urban Microorganisms
    VISIBLE/INVISIBLE

    THE URBAN DICHOTOMY

    How cities manage life through perception: what's visible gets excluded, what's invisible gets ignored. Pigeons represent the violence of visibility, microorganisms represent the erasure of invisibility.

    CORE IDEA

    Both species experience urban violence, but through different mechanisms: one through spikes and noise, the other through chemicals and sterilization. Together, they map the full spectrum of urban exclusion.
